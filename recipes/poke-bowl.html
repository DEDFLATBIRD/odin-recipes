Poke Bowl

Meal prep Poke bowl

Hawaiian dish of marinated raw fish or seafood served over rice and topped with various ingredients

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Edamame
  • Carrots
  • Nuts
  • Seeds
  • Fried shallots
  • Roasted garlic
  • Spring onions
  • Jalapeños
  • Chili oil
  • White rice

Steps

  1. Mix all ingridients in a regular size bowl.
  2. On a second bowl, add white rice until half bowl is full.
  3. Add previous ingridients from the first bowl to the second bowl filled with rice.
  4. Ready to eat!!!
