Nigiri Salmon Sushi

Nigiri style sushi with salmon on top with soy sauce and wasabi on the side.

Ingredients

  • Calrose rise
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salmon steak
  • Wasabi
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice until the water becomes cloudy. Drain and repeat 4 to 5 times.
  2. In a saucepan over high heat, bring the rice, water and salt to a boil.
  3. Spread the cooked rice onto a baking sheet and pour the vinegar mixture overtop.
  4. With a sharp knife, trim and discard the discoloured sections of the salmon.
  5. Shape the rice into oblong balls each. Spread a small amount of wasabni onto each rice ball and cover with a salmon slice.
  6. Serve with the soy sauce and pickled ginger.
  7. Ready to eat!!!
