Takoyaki

Takoyaki appetizer

Japanese street food of bite-sized balls made from batter and filled with various ingredients, typically octopus, and cooked in a special pan.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Dashi
  • Dried kelp
  • Bonito flakes
  • Diced octopus
  • Tempura scraps
  • Pickled ginger
  • Green onion

Steps

  1. Mix all ingridients well until getting creamy texture.
  2. Pour mix and fill with diced octopus on special takoyaki pan.
  3. Flip takoyaki balls constantly until ball is fully cooked.
  4. Add sauce once takoyaki balls are cooked and served.
  5. Ready to eat!!!
