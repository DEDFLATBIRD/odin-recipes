Takoyaki

Takoyaki appetizer

Japanese street food of bite-sized balls made from batter and filled with various ingredients, typically octopus, and cooked in a special pan.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Dashi
  • Dried kelp
  • Bonito flakes
  • Diced octopus
  • Tempura scraps
  • Pickled ginger
  • Green onion

Steps

  1. Mix
  2. Prep
  3. Pour and fill
  4. Flip
  5. Sauce
